# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in LABLOG a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

Positive behavior includes demonstrating empathy, respecting different
viewpoints, giving and accepting constructive feedback, taking responsibility,
and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or attention, trolling,
insults, personal attacks, harassment, publishing private information without
permission, or other conduct that is inappropriate in a professional community.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and
other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project owner through the
contact method on their GitHub profile. Reports will be handled promptly and
with respect for the reporter's privacy and safety.
